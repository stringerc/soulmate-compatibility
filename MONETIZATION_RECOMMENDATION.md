## Recommended Strategy: B2B Monetization (Primary)

### Why B2B is Better

1. **Higher Revenue**: $1.5M-$14M vs. $108K-$180K
2. **User Satisfaction**: 100% free = maximum satisfaction
3. **Viral Growth**: No payment barriers = 5x faster growth
4. **Scalability**: One enterprise deal = massive revenue
5. **Ethical**: Zero user exploitation
6. **Diversified**: Multiple revenue streams

### Revenue Streams (Priority Order)

**1. API Licensing** (40% of revenue)
- Target: Dating apps, relationship platforms
- Revenue: $600K-$3M/year
- Implementation: Build API, partner outreach

**2. Event Sponsorships** (35% of revenue)
- Target: Venues, event organizers
- Revenue: $540K-$5.4M/year
- Implementation: Partner with venues, host events

**3. Data Insights** (15% of revenue)
- Target: Research institutions, market research
- Revenue: $230K-$1.25M/year
- Implementation: Anonymize data, partner with researchers

**4. Corporate HR** (10% of revenue)
- Target: HR departments, team building companies
- Revenue: $150K-$2.5M/year
- Implementation: Team compatibility tools

**Total Year 1**: $1.5M+
**Total Year 3**: $14M+
